Definitions for shr-core-Dosage-model.

shr-core-Dosage-model
Definition

The dosage of the medication. The data structure is flexible and can describe a dosage prescribed, administered, or reported.

Control0..*
shr-core-Dosage-model.doseAmount[x]
Definition

The amount of medication taken per dose, as a quantity or range. Relative dose (e.g., per kg body weight or surface area) are indicated by specifying appropriate units (e.g., mg/kg or mg/m2 etc).
