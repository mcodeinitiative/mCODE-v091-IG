TNMPathologicRegionalNodesClassification

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/shr/StructureDefinition/oncocore-TNMPathologicRegionalNodesClassification

Classification of the presence or absence of metastases in regional lymph nodes, assessed through pathologic analysis of a specimen.

This profile builds on Observation.

This profile was published on Mon Apr 08 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation.
