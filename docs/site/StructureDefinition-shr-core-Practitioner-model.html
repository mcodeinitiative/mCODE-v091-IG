Practitioner

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/shr/StructureDefinition/shr-core-Practitioner-model

A person who practices a healing art. HumanName is limited to one required value by US-Core. Gender is required by US-Core. US-Core required an identifier, either a NPI (preferred) or tax id.

This logical model was published on Wed May 01 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation.
