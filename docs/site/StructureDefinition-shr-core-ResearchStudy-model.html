ResearchStudy

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/shr/StructureDefinition/shr-core-ResearchStudy-model

A clinical trial or similar research study. Reference: list of FDA data elements describing a research study: prsinfo.clinicaltrials.gov/definitions.html

This logical model was published on Wed May 01 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation.
