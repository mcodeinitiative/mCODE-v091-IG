QuestionnaireResponse

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/shr/StructureDefinition/shr-core-QuestionnaireResponse-model

A structured set of questions and their answers. The questions are ordered and grouped into coherent subsets, corresponding to the structure of the grouping of the questionnaire being responded to.

This logical model was published on Tue May 07 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation.
