  <p>A person in the role of a patient. Sometimes, the patient is not the subject of information in a clinical statement where the Patient is the SubjectOfRecord.

SHR follows Argonaut and makes the value set binding on marital status required. SHR also adds optional extensions on communication ability to more fully characterize language skills.
MaritalStatus has a required binding in Argonaut, but an extensible binding in US-Core. To be feasible under both DSTU2 Argonaut and STU3 US-Core, the required binding strength is adopted.</p>
