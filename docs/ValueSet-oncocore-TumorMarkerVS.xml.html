<p><b>TumorMarkerVS ValueSet</b></p>
<p>A tumor marker is a substance found in tissue or blood or other body fluids that may be a sign of cancer or certain benign (noncancer) conditions. This value set of LOINC codes will include a comprehensive list of tumor markers across all cancers, and regardless of method or specimen type.
