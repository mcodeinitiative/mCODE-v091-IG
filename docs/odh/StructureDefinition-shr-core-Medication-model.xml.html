  <p><b>Medication Logical Model</b></p>
  <p>A type of prescription drug or over-the-counter drug that is used to prevent, treat, or relieve symptoms of a disease or abnormal condition, but excluding vaccines.
In FHIR DSTU2 and STU3, Medication lacks the identifier attribute, which is included in SHR as an extension.
DSTU2 lacks the status attribute, which is required by US-Core.
Batch is 0..1 in FHIR R4, and accordingly is restricted here.</p>
