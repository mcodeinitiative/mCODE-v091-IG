WoundUnderminingAssessment

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/shr/StructureDefinition/sw-WoundUnderminingAssessment-model

Assessment of deep tissue (subcutaneous fat and muscle) damage around the wound margin. The undermining length and clock direction arrays must be the same length, and the order in the arrays is significant*; the elements at the same array position shall be interpreted as pairs.

This logical model was published on Sat Mar 30 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a draft by HL7 Patient Care and Clinical Information Modeling Initiative Work Groups.
