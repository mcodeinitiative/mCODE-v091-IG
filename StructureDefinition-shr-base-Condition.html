Condition

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/fhirURL/StructureDefinition/shr-base-Condition

A condition that is or may be present in a subject. 'Condition' is interpreted broadly and could be a disorder, abnormality, problem, injury, complaint, functionality, illness, disease, ailment, sickness, affliction, upset, difficulty, disorder, symptom, worry, or trouble.

This profile builds on http://fhir.org/guides/argonaut/StructureDefinition/argo-condition.

This profile was published on Thu Feb 07 00:00:00 EST 2019 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation.
