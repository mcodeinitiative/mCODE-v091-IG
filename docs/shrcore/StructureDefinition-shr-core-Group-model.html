Group

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/shr/StructureDefinition/shr-core-Group-model

A set of entities (personnel, material, or places) to be considered together. May be a pool of like-type resources, a team, or combination of personnel, material and places.

This logical model was published on Wed May 01 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation.
