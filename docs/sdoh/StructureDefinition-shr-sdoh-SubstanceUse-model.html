SubstanceUse

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/shr/StructureDefinition/shr-sdoh-SubstanceUse-model

Assertion concerning the past or current use of a substance (alcohol, recreational drugs, illegal drugs, or abuse of prescription medication) that could negatively impact the subject’s health.

This logical model was published on Mon Apr 01 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a draft by MITRE Corporation.
