TNMPathologicPrimaryTumorClassification

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/shr/StructureDefinition/oncocore-TNMPathologicPrimaryTumorClassification

Classification of the primary tumor, based on its size and extent, assessed through pathologic analysis of a tumor specimen.

This profile builds on Observation.

This profile was published on Wed Mar 13 00:00:00 EDT 2019 as a draft by The MITRE Corporation.
